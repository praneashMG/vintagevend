Our Story

Saving Vending History, One Machine at a Time

Founded in 2010 by a collector and a master technician, VintageVend began in a small garage with a single rusted 1950s Vendo. Today, we’re the most trusted name in vintage vending restoration, serving collectors, museums, and retro cafes across the globe.

“We don’t just fix machines — we preserve the soul of mid‑century America.”

— Marcus Cole, Founder

2010
Founded

Our Mission

To rescue, restore, and rehome vintage vending machines with authenticity and craftsmanship, ensuring these icons of American design live on for generations.

Authenticity above all – original parts and finishes
Transparent process, weekly updates, fair pricing
Lifetime support for every machine we restore
